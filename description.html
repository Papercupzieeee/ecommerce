About GroupBuy

About the GroupBuy feature and know how it works


GroupBuy is feature allows user to purchase products at lower price by buying
together as a group.Instead of purchasing individually, users can join or create a group to unlock exclusive
discounts once the required number of participants is reached.


  1. A user starts a Group Buy for a product.
  2. Other users can join the same group within a limited time
  3. When the minimum group size is reached,the order is confirmed
  4. If the group is not completed within the time limit,the order is automaticaaly cancelled.
  5. If you are the person who shares link to your friends, your friends will get a half discount as you
    For exmaple : If you receive 50% of Discount means, your friend will get 25% of discount

Benefits of GroupBuy


  • Lower prices compared to individual purchases
  • Encourages community and shared shopping
  • Ideal for bulk and high demanded products
